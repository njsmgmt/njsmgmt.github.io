What it is

A done-for-you outbound engine.

Apex is a managed prospecting system, not software you have to learn. We build it, configure it to your ideal customer profile, and hand you a system that runs every working day. You don't touch a line of code. You handle the conversations it starts.

How it works

Three steps. Run for you.

01

Prospect & connect

Every working day, Apex identifies decision-makers that match your ICP and starts reaching out on your behalf.

02

Research & message

When someone connects, Apex researches a live signal such as a recent post, a funding round, or a launch, and your first message references it.

03

Track & follow up

Every touch logs to a live dashboard. Follow-ups go out to anyone who hasn't replied. Nothing falls through the cracks.

How we work together

A managed engagement.

Apex is a managed service, not a product you buy off a shelf. Engagements begin with an initial 90-day term, which is the build-and-iterate period where we set the system up to your ICP, launch outreach, and tune the targeting and messaging based on what lands. After that, the engagement continues month to month and you can cancel with 30 days notice.

The only ongoing infrastructure cost is a Claude Max subscription, about $100 a month, paid to Anthropic directly with no markup. We cover scope and pricing on the intro call.

What you need

Three things.

01

Somewhere for it to run

Apex runs on a dedicated, always-on machine, not your work computer. We provide a pre-configured Mac Mini as part of the engagement, or run it on a spare Mac you already have. You manage everything from a web dashboard that works on any device, including the PCs your company uses.

02

An active LinkedIn profile

A real profile with history and credibility. Your profile does the selling. Apex generates the conversations that lead people to it.

03

A Claude Max subscription

About $100 a month, paid to Anthropic directly. This powers all signal research and messaging. Cancel anytime.

Privacy Policy

Effective June 2, 2026

This policy explains how NJS MGMT LLC ("NJS MGMT," "we," "us") handles information in connection with the Apex service and this website. By booking a call or working with us, you agree to this policy.

We use it to set up and operate the service, communicate with you, handle billing, and improve how the service works. We do not sell your information.

Each handles data under its own terms and only as needed to provide its part of the service.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet legal and accounting obligations. When an engagement ends, we return or delete your campaign data on request.
